About

Let me introduce myself.

I'm Emily Kerr, a historian with a passion for public history and digital communication. With a lifetime of historical work, volunteer and otherwise, under my belt, I thrive on making history accessible to individuals of any age or background. My commitment to educating communities is evident in my successful collaboration on projects like the 828 Digital Archives for Historical Equity. Eager to contribute my skills to further public history education, I am dedicated to continuous growth and excellence. Let's connect and explore how my experience aligns with your needs!

Profile

I have a passion for local history and am currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Digital Public History for that very reason.
